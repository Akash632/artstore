About the Artist

Hi, I'm Akash, an Engineering Student and an Artist based out of Andhra Pradesh, India. I've always been inclined towards art ever since I was a child. What started as a hobby during school turned into my biggest passion during college. Haven't looked back ever since. I love capturing my surroundings in the paintings I create. Nature, my garden, skies from our terrace to name a few. Now you can get your hands on them in the form of Sketchbooks, Notebooks, Art prints and Stickers. Original paintings too! Having a brand of my own with a line of art and stationery products has always been a dream and it's hard to believe that it's finally happening! I hope you find something you like here. So glad to have you on board in my journey :)
